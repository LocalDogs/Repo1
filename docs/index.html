What is Local Dogs?

  • Local Dogs is an Android app designed to let pet owners match their pet with another user's pet, based on certain criteria.
  • We stand out apart from the competition by dedicating the app to exclusively setting up dog playdates or get-togethers. You can swipe through a queue of other pets, so you can find the perfect match!
    • You can filter dogs by:
      • Breed
      • Age
      • Weight
      • Activity Level
  • Once you find your perfect match, use the integrated Google Maps and set up a playdate through contacting your match's owner!

  • With Local Dogs being a social app, we want to ensure that our users maintain safe practices when talking to strangers.
    • Our Terms of Use is a binding agreement where a user understands that any interactions outside of the app is beyond the control of Local Dogs, LLC.
    • Any actions deemed inappropriate by the standards of the Terms of Use and Local Dogs team will result in pressing actions that may resolve in an account ban.
    • We take the safety of our users seriously, and we expect that users make the smart decisions necessary to ensure their safety!

Goal Functionality

  • Create a profile for your dog, specifying name, age, breed, weight, activity level, etc.
  • Swipe through our Cardstack to match with other dogs based on preferences!
  • Contact the owner of the dog you matched with to set up a playdate between your dog and the matched dog.
  • Find a suitable area for the playdate with the integration of Google Maps in our app!
